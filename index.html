ShutterBuddy

ShutterBuddy is an web app that enables you to build the photo album of your dreams -- no camera required! For our final project at The Iron Yard, I combined the Flickr and Google Maps APIs with a little Rails magic to help you create a customized, Creative Commons-licensed photo album based on location, landmarks, weather and time of day. At the end, you get a link to your album to share your fabulous trip with friends and family!
