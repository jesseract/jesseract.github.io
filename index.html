ShutterBuddy

ShutterBuddy is an app that enables you to build the photo album of your dreams -- no camera required! For our final project at The Iron Yard, we combined the Flickr and Google Maps APIs with a little rails magic to help you create a customized, Creative Commons-licensed photo album based on location, landmarks, weather and time of day.
